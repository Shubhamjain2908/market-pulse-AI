# Market Pulse AI — Domain Glossary

Terminology used across architecture, code, and documentation. These definitions
are the single source of truth; every document and PR should use them consistently.

## Runs

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Decision Run** | Scheduled workflow that may generate investment analysis and admit new paper trades. Typically the 08:45 IST weekday run. |
| **EOD Reconciliation Run** | Post-close refresh that reconciles market data and evaluates existing positions without admitting new trades. Typically the 16:30 IST weekday run. |

## Portfolio Analysis

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Proposed Action** | Portfolio action (HOLD/ADD/TRIM/EXIT) before deterministic guardrails are applied. Reflects the model's raw recommendation. |
| **Effective Action** | Authoritative action after deterministic guardrails (strategy rules, concentration limits, technical escalation). Displayed to the user; may differ from Proposed Action. |

## Paper Trades

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Trade Status** | Lifecycle state of a paper trade: `OPEN`, `CLOSED_WIN`, `CLOSED_LOSS`, `CLOSED_TIME`. Controls dedup for new entries. |
| **Pricing State** | Whether an active paper trade has a quote for the expected market session: `PRICED` (fresh data available) or `UNPRICED` (no new bars). Separate from Trade Status — an UNPRICED trade remains OPEN. |

## Theses

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Research Thesis** | Analysis card generated by the LLM for a candidate symbol. May appear in the briefing even when admission is blocked. |
| **Admission Decision** | Deterministic result deciding whether a thesis may create a paper trade row. Gated by confidence, path confirmation, earnings blackout, and operating quality checks. |
